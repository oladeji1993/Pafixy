WHO WE ARE

At Payfixy we help you build and grow your business through our multiple seamless payment solutions. We offer all the digital payment solutions your business needs to thrive. Following a successful transition process, Payfixy has taken responsibility of all payment solutions previously managed by its sister company Innovate1Pay. Our mission is to provide access to lifestyle and payment services for public/ private sectors and individuals through our World Class Platforms.

Payfixy Limited is an indigenous Nigerian company duly registered as a limited liability company.

Our business objectives include the deployment of a secure payment platform with multiple applications
and functionalities in furtherance of the provision of digital payment services for applicants/consumers
within the e-payments space.

Our company is a CBN-licensed Payment Service Solution Provider (PSSP) and a
major provider of digital and electronic payment processing services in Nigeria with
millions of client transactions and payments processed in Nigeria on our platform.

We are currently engaged in the provision of payment solution services through our
affiliate company (SW Global Limited) which provides cutting-edge technology
services for the implementation of the following projects in Nigeria:

Nigeria Driver’s License
Nigerian Passport and Visa Processing and Issuance
NMCN (Nurses and Midwives)
Registration and Payments
Council for the Regulation of Freight

Forwarding in Nigeria (CRFFN) Registration and Import Payment and Processing

Our payment platform is customized for customers’ needs with multiple payment
channels to ensure ease of payments and is positioned as one of the largest Nigerian
payment platforms in Nigeria.
